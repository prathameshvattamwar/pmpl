FAQs

General

  • I am unable to verify my phone number.

    Phone number verification is not required to use the app at this time.

Bus

  • How many buses are trackable?

    Currently, 1,200 buses are trackable. Additional buses are being added daily.

  • What is the frequency of location updates?

    Location updates are refreshed every 30 seconds.

  • Why is no bus showing on the app in my area?

    This may occur due to connectivity issues or if no buses are active in your area.

Pass

  • Can I book a pass in advance?

    Yes, passes can be booked up to 7 days in advance.

  • My transaction is completed, but the pass is still showing as pending. What should I do?

    Please wait for a few minutes. If the issue persists, contact support.

Tickets

  • What is the validity of a ticket?

    Tickets are valid for 24 hours from the time of booking.

  • Can I book a ticket in advance?

    Yes, tickets can be booked up to 48 hours in advance.
